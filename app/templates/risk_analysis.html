💡 Risk Probability vs Causal Effect — What's the difference?
Risk Probability (ML Model)
P(CHD = 1 | patient features). A correlation-based score. High-risk patients share features with CHD patients in training data. Does not tell you why or what causes the risk.
ITE — Individual Treatment Effect (Causal)
E[CHD if smoke − CHD if not smoke | this patient]. A causal estimate — how much smoking changes this specific patient's CHD risk after controlling for all confounders.
